Atelier Stockholm was born out of love for fashion. Created by two young multi-cultural sisters with multicultural backgrounds whose dream is to find clothes that fits and includes everyone regardless of gender, background and body type. All collections are created in a limited edition to avoid excessive production. The goal for us is to always come up with stylish and classic garments which lasts for many seasons.

Designed in Sweden and produced in France.

The iconic trio

The french craftmanship that is instiled in the Alienné, Eloise and Juliette sets the tone of a dark but conventional style that has the ability to be adapted into your daily outfits in more ways than imagined.

Pouffé

The pouffé blouse is the dreamy and classic blouse from our core collection to have in your warderobe. Wether it is to match to a work outfit or a late night. The ability to adjust the sleeves makes this blouse the perfect versatile piece in your warderobe to wear.

Alienné

The Alienné is a classic cropped blazer and high waisted pants that is set to work as an intricate addition to your minimalistic warderobe. It comes in two colours, black and khaki and is created in a basic style that still has the possibility to elivate your outfit.

Biscuit & Bleu

Our biscuit blouse and bleu jeans are the classic pieces for the trendsetter to own. With the recycled materials and fibers that has been used to create both of these intricate pieces it is to no suprise as to why it has become a favorite amongst instagrammers.

Freya

The Freya blouse is by far the most delicate piece in our collection core. With materials such as satin and silk threads it is no surprise why this piece is a favorite amongst business ladies that are searching for the perfect white blouse to wear for the office hours. No wonder!
